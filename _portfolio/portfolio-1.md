## StatsStockX – NBA Player Securities Trading Platform
**Technologies:** Python, TypeScript, React, WebSocket | **Duration:** Oct. 2025 – Present

### Overview
Built a full-stack securities trading platform where NBA players function as tradable assets, combining sports analytics with financial engineering principles.

### Key Features
- **Quantitative Pricing Algorithm:** Developed time-series models achieving 78% prediction accuracy for player valuations
- **Order Matching Engine:** Engineered with O(log n) complexity using red-black trees, handling 100+ concurrent users
- **Real-time Infrastructure:** WebSocket-based price feeds, order book management, and multi-asset portfolio construction tools
- **Options Trading:** Implemented Black-Scholes pricing for calls and puts on player securities
- **Risk Analytics:** Calculated Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega) for hedging strategies
- **Portfolio Management:** Built analytics tracking Sharpe ratio, maximum drawdown, and 95% Value-at-Risk

### Technical Implementation
The platform demonstrates advanced concepts in quantitative finance, including derivative pricing, risk management, and high-frequency trading infrastructure, all applied to the novel domain of sports player securities.
